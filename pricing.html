FAQ

Can I renew my listing after 6 months?
Yes. Free listings expire after 6 months and show a red Expired badge. Contact us on Nostr to renew. Verified and Featured listings last 12 months.
What counts as proof of payment?
A screenshot of the completed Lightning payment or the payment hash is sufficient.
Can I upgrade from Free to Verified later?
Yes, at any time. Just send the payment and contact us on Nostr.
Is there a refund policy?
Due to the nature of Lightning payments, we cannot offer refunds. If your listing is rejected for violating our guidelines, we will notify you before charging.
