Nostr Market Directory is an independent, community-curated index of marketplace projects, independent creators and local communities building on the Nostr protocol.

What is this?

The Nostr marketplace ecosystem is growing fast but remains scattered. New projects launch without visibility, creators have no shared home, and local communities are invisible outside their own Signal groups.

This directory exists to fix that. Whether you are looking for a place to buy or sell, a creator worth supporting, or a local community of like-minded people near you – this is the place to start.

FAQ

How do I submit my project or profile?
Click the + Submit button on the main page. Fill in the form and submit. Your listing appears immediately after passing basic validation.
What does Verified mean?
Verified means we confirmed the listing is a real, active project or person – not a fake, scam or duplicate. It does not mean we personally endorse the listing.
How long does a listing stay in the directory?
Listings are active for 6 months. Two weeks before expiry, the listing owner receives a reminder. Renewing keeps the listing in the directory.
Can I report a listing?
Yes. Every card has a Report button. Reports are reviewed by the admin. We remove listings that involve illegal goods, scams, fraud or spam.
Does the directory store my personal data?
We store only what you submit in the listing form. No tracking, no cookies, no analytics.
Can I pay with Bitcoin Lightning?
Yes. Verified and Featured tiers are payable via Lightning. We also accept donations on our Lightning address.
I found outdated or incorrect information. What should I do?
Use the Report button and select "Other". Describe the issue and we will review and update the listing.
